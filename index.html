About Parasdham Youth Ahmedabad

Parasdham Youth Ahmedabad is a dedicated NGO driven by the spirit of selfless service, inspired by the teachings and guidance of Rashtrasant Param Gurudev Shree Namramuni Maharajsaheb. Rooted in compassion and a deep sense of community, our mission is to uplift and empower individuals through acts of sevacreating lasting positive change in society. With a focus on social welfare, education, and spiritual growth, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need, fostering a culture of kindness, generosity, and unity. Join us in our journey to spread love and light through service.

So join us in this beautiful journey of endless seva and smiles.
